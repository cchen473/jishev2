# National Emergency Response Protocols

## Section 4.2: Urban Structural Collapse
In the event of a structural collapse within urban limits:
1. Establish a 500-meter safety perimeter immediately.
2. Deploy structural engineering teams to assess stability.
3. Activate heavy rescue units (USAR Task Force).
4. If fire is present, prioritize fire suppression before entry, unless immediate life threat exists.

## Section 5.1: Flood Response
For urban flooding exceeding 1 meter depth:
1. Deploy amphibious vehicles and boats.
2. Establish high-ground evacuation centers.
3. Monitor electrical grid integrity and shut down substations in flooded zones.
4. Priority evacuation for hospitals and elderly care facilities.

## Section 6.3: Wildfire Containment
For wildfires near residential zones:
1. Establish firebreaks at least 30 meters wide.
2. Evacuate residents within a 5km radius of the predicted fire path.
3. Deploy aerial water tankers for hotspot suppression.
4. Ground crews to focus on protecting critical infrastructure.

# 国家应急响应协议 (中文版)

## 第 4.2 节：城市结构坍塌
如果城市范围内发生结构坍塌：
1. 立即建立 500 米的安全警戒线。
2. 部署结构工程团队评估稳定性。
3. 启动重型救援队（城市搜救特遣队）。
4. 如果有火情，除非存在直接生命威胁，否则应优先灭火后再进入。

## 第 5.1 节：洪水响应 (Flood Response)
针对深度超过 1 米的城市内涝或洪水：
1. 部署两栖车辆和救援艇。
2. 建立高地疏散中心。
3. 监控电网完整性，关闭被淹没区域的变电站。
4. 优先疏散医院和养老院人员。

## 第 6.3 节：野火控制
针对居民区附近的野火：
1. 建立至少 30 米宽的防火隔离带。
2. 疏散预计火势蔓延路径半径 5 公里内的居民。
3. 部署空中洒水飞机进行热点压制。
4. 地面人员集中精力保护关键基础设施。
